Permanent Mounts

Pull the release, slide your case out, and you're free. When you're ready to lock back in, slide it up the rail and click it into place. The Trail Rail mounts directly to the bottom of most Pelican case models and installs onto any roof rack in minutes. Your gear goes where you go, not where it's bolted.

The Brand

Made for the
Overland World

Trail Rail was born out of a simple frustration. Permanently bolting a case to your truck means it lives there. We built a better way. The Trail Rail mounts to the bottom of your Pelican case using provided waterproof bolts and washers, installs straight onto your roof rack, and lets you pull the release and slide your case off in seconds. Slide it back up and click it in when you're ready to roll. No tools, no hassle, no compromise on weatherproofing.
